Rosemary Steak Recipes

This is a wonderful sterak dish that was prepared for me when I lived in Argentina. This Argentinean steak dish is quite popular in various restaurants around Buenos Aires.


Ingredents

  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 4 New York strip steaks, cut 1-inch thick

Recipe instructions

  1. Combined the red wine, salt and reosemary in a small bowl. Let stand at room temperature for 2 to 3 hours.
  2. Heat large griddle or cast-iron skillet over high heat. Place the steaks on the hot pan, and cook for about 8 minutes per side, or to desired degree of doneness. The internal temperature should be at least 145 degrees F (62 degrees C) for medium rare. Pour in the wine mixture, and allow it to boil for a minute. Serve steaks with sauce on a deep platter.
